GCdocs

GCdocs is the official Electronic Document and Records Management Solution (EDRMS) of the Government of Canada (GC) as well as the tool that organizations use to create, collect and preserve information up to and including “Protected B” sensitivity. It is also used as a collaborative tool by easily sharing, organizing, evaluating, identifying and disposing of information.

GCdocs is an Information Management initiative.
