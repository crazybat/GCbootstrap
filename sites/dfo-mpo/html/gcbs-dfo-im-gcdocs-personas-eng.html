Personas

Personas represent the typical user types that are using a site, software and/or application.

Although personas are usually based on fictional characters, these are based on real users within DFO and the Canadian Coast Guard. The following personas will help you better understand:

  • Who the users are
  • What type of role they play
  • What primary tasks are performed within GCdocs

Nerissa Javier

Information Administrator

User Type(s):

  • Controller
  • Contributor
  • Consumer

Nerissa Javier: Controller, Contributor, Consumer

Nerissa's primary use of GCdocs is for Records management: reviewing lists for validation, keeping track of physical items and related disposition forms.

In addition to this, Nerissa also provides assistance to the GCdocs users in the Pacific Region by providing a suite of IM-related services and training.
