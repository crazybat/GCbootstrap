Innovation Fair 2019 – Lead and Coordinator

In December, the BCFC Secretariat will send out the official recruitment call for a lead and a coordinator for the 2019 Innovation Fair. These will be two 6-month full-time micro-missions beginning in January 2019. These positions entail an extraordinary opportunity to work with executives, other levels of government, industry, and academia, while being part of a national team under the leadership of the Privy Council Office. Interested candidates should start seeking their manager’s support in preparation for the call-out. Preliminary expressions of interest or questions can be sent to the BCFC Secretariat.
